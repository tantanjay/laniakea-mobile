# Emotion, Language, and Numbers
*A Critical Clarification*

Laniakea does **not** claim to measure human emotion itself.

This distinction is fundamental.

> **Emotion is not being reduced to numbers.**  
> **Numbers are used to approximate patterns in language that correlate with emotional states.**

What the system operates on is **language**, not lived experience.

Nothing inside Laniakea:
- feels emotion
- experiences emotion
- understands emotion
- or “has” emotion

Instead, the on-device model recognizes **statistical structure** in how humans *express* emotions through words, phrasing, and narrative patterns.

---

## What the Vibe Score Represents (and What It Does Not)

The Vibe Score is:
- a **geometric projection** in semantic embedding space
- a **relative signal**, not an absolute judgment
- a reflection of how closely a journal entry’s language aligns with previously observed expressions of *joy* or *distress*

The Vibe Score is **not**:
- a psychological diagnosis
- a clinical assessment
- a claim about internal mental state
- a replacement for human self-reflection

It is best understood as a **measurement instrument**, similar to a thermometer:
- A thermometer does not *experience* warmth
- It measures a physical proxy (molecular motion) that correlates with how warmth is felt
- Likewise, Laniakea measures linguistic structure that correlates with how emotion is commonly expressed

---

## Why This Still Feels Meaningful

Human language is not random.

Across cultures and individuals, emotional states tend to influence:
- word choice
- sentence length
- certainty vs. rumination
- agency vs. helplessness
- temporal framing (past-focused vs. future-oriented)

Modern sentence embedding models capture these regularities implicitly during training.  
Laniakea does not invent emotional meaning — it **reads out structure that already exists** in how people write.

---

## Design Philosophy

Because of this understanding, Laniakea intentionally:
- avoids hard labels (“happy”, “sad”, “depressed”)
- emphasizes trends over time rather than single entries
- keeps all interpretation **local, private, and user-owned**
- treats the Vibe Score as a **mirror**, not a verdict

The goal is **self-awareness**, not classification.

---

## Ethical Boundary

Laniakea is designed for **personal reflection**, not external judgment.

Any interpretation of the Vibe Score should always defer to:
- the user’s own understanding of their experience
- human context
- lived reality beyond text

Numbers can reveal patterns — but meaning remains human.
